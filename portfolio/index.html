Frequently Asked Questions

Does the AI creative look obviously fake? +
No. We use the latest AI models and custom prompting to generate assets that look real enough to convert — UGC-quality content, lifestyle product shots, and spokesperson-style videos. If something looks off-brand, we revise it before delivery.
What types of ads can you produce? +
AI UGC-style video ads, lifestyle product visuals, spokesperson content, static image ads, and branded motion graphics — across fashion, beauty, food, real estate, finance, and general e-commerce.
What do you need from us to get started? +
High-quality product images, your brand assets (logo, fonts, colour palette), and access to any existing ad performance data. We handle the rest.
How long does it take to get the first assets? +
2–3 weeks from signed agreement to first delivery. Week 1 is onboarding and strategy. Weeks 2–3 are production. Urgent turnarounds available on request.
Can I request revisions? +
Yes. Every project includes revision rounds to ensure brand alignment before we finalise delivery. Our goal is assets you're proud to run, not 'good enough.'
